# Overview

Grid stability relies on highly accurate load forecasting. Overestimating demand leads to wasted energy and higher costs, while underestimating causes brownouts. This project builds a robust time-series forecasting pipeline to predict electricity demand based on historical usage and external regressors.

## The Architecture

* **Data Processing:** Cleaned and resampled high-frequency grid data using `pandas`, handling missing values and anomalies in the time-series.
* **Modeling:** Evaluated multiple forecasting algorithms, including classical statistical methods (ARIMA) and modern additive models (Facebook Prophet).
* **Evaluation:** Optimized for Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) to ensure high reliability across both peak and off-peak hours.
