# Overview

Food blogs and historical texts are notorious for burying recipes under paragraphs of unstructured narrative. *Recipe Archaeologist* acts as a targeted extraction engine, parsing raw text and outputting clean, structured JSON containing ingredients, measurements, and step-by-step instructions.

## The Architecture

* **Scraping & Parsing:** Utilized `BeautifulSoup` to navigate DOM trees and strip away boilerplate web content.
* **Natural Language Processing:** Implemented `Spacy` for Named Entity Recognition (NER) to identify quantities, units, and food items, successfully converting colloquial measurements into standardized data formats.
